Frequently Asked Questions

How does Casho app work?

Casho is a pioneering app that lets users monetize their personal data. After downloading, users can link various data sources like shopping receipts and bank transactions. With explicit consent, Casho fetches this data, offering users rewards for sharing select insights. It's a seamless way to gain control over your data and earn from it.

What data does Casho collect?

Casho prioritizes user consent and transparency in data collection. With your explicit permission, we gather information on your spending habits, device specifics, browsing patterns, and geolocation details. For a comprehensive understanding, please refer to our privacy policy.

Why do businesses want my data?

Businesses seek your data to gain insights into consumer behavior, refine their marketing strategies, drive research & development, and ultimately boost sales.

How does Casho make money?

Casho earns revenue by offering businesses access to ethically-sourced consumer data. Through the platform, businesses can derive insights from anonymized consumer data and prevailing market trends, and they pay for this valuable information. This enables companies to make informed decisions
